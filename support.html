Billing Issues

If you're facing issues with billing, follow these steps:

  • Check your transaction history to identify any discrepancies.
  • Review your payment method for any errors.
  • If the issue persists, contact customer support for further assistance.

If you need additional help, feel free to contact us here.

Network Problems

If you're having network issues, follow these steps:

  • Restart your device to ensure it's connected to the network properly.
  • Check for any ongoing service outages in your area by visiting our status page.
  • Try resetting your network settings if the problem persists.
  • If none of the above work, try connecting to a different Wi-Fi network or use mobile data.

If you're still facing issues, you can reach out to support here for further troubleshooting.

Account Management

If you're having trouble with your account, here are some things to check:

  • Ensure your personal details are up to date in the account settings.
  • If you've forgotten your password, try the password recovery process.
  • If you're facing issues with account verification, check your email for verification links or follow the verification guide.
